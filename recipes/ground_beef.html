Ground Beef with Potatoes

It is practical. It is delicious. It allows different combinations and variations. Let see how it's done.

    You will need:
  • 1,5kg of grounf beef
  • Salt
  • Potatoes of your liking
  • Tomatoes, specially naked tomatoes (pomodori pelati)
  • Spices (consider herbs and pepper)
  • Onions
    How to prepare:
  1. Short version: put it all together in a pan, put the pan on the stove, and forget about it.
  2. (Not so) long version: put the ground beef with salt in a pan, and add a little bit of water. No oil needed.
  3. Slice your potatoes the way you prefer and add them, together with the spices.
  4. Wait for sometime (maybe 15 minutes) and add sliced onions and tomatoes.
  5. Keep mixing everything from time to time until the meat and the potatoes are cooked.
  6. Of course you can make it all with more ingredients and refinement, but that's enough.
  7. Pro tip: add parmesan cheese when it's hot.
