Barbecue - Beef Ribs

Who does not like some well cooked and fat beef rib? Well, some people don't. But we do, so here is a very simple recipe to prepare a good barbecue for you and your friends.

    Here is the list of ingredients:
  • Meat
  • Salt
  • Anything else (like spices) if you want to have more work to do.
    How to prepare:
  1. First, you put salt all over the piece of meat you have. Since we're talking about beef ribs, that's what you put salt on.
  2. After that, you take everything to your grill or whatever you are using to barbecue your beefs.
  3. Wait long enough, turn the beefs a few times, and you're good to go (meaning the beefs are good to be eaten; you can stay).
